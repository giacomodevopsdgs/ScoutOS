# Use Cases

ScoutOS is intended to monitor opportunities that are easy to miss or tedious to compare manually.

## Shopping Deals

- Watch saved searches and product pages.
- Detect discounts, price drops, and rare listings.
- Rank by price, condition, seller quality, distance, and urgency.

## Job Openings

- Track role searches across company boards and job platforms.
- Rank by skill fit, compensation signals, location, seniority, and application friction.
- Detect reposts and stale listings.

## Flights and Travel

- Monitor routes, dates, lodging, and packages.
- Rank by price, timing, stops, flexibility, and total trip constraints.
- Detect sudden fare drops.

## Real Estate

- Track sale and rental listings.
- Rank by price, location, size, commute, amenities, and listing freshness.
- Monitor changes in price and availability.

## Events

- Track concerts, conferences, meetups, and local events.
- Rank by relevance, schedule fit, location, price, and scarcity.

## Price Tracking

- Maintain watched items over time.
- Store price snapshots.
- Alert when thresholds or ranking criteria are met.

# ScoutOS Manifesto

ScoutOS exists to reduce repetitive searching and improve decision-making.

It is not a bot for clicking around the web. It is a personal intelligence system for scouting opportunities, evaluating trade-offs, and helping decide when to act and when to wait.

## What ScoutOS Believes

- Not every search should end with a purchase or action.
- The best recommendation can be to wait.
- Value is more important than price.
- Sustainability and durability matter.
- Explanations matter more than opaque scores.
- Automation should reduce noise, not create more of it.
- AI should support judgment, not replace it.

## What ScoutOS Should Become

A modular platform where independent Scouts can discover and evaluate opportunities across different domains:

- shopping
- jobs
- travel
- real estate
- events
- learning
- investments

The long-term goal is to build a trustworthy assistant that helps evaluate options according to explicit personal principles, structured evidence, and transparent reasoning.
